Icecream

Scoops Icecream Cakes

Scoops

We make our delicious homemade gelato ice cream with lots of love and utmost care. Our ice cream is made daily on site in small batches. That allows us to provide with the funkiest and the freshest taste. We have created a variety of flavours ranging from the traditional Vanilla to Mint Choc Chip to many more exotic flavour of ice cream.

Biscoff Ice Cream

Vegan biscoff ice cream is made with oat milk, organic cane sugar, topped with lotus sauce and lotus crumbs

Coconut Oreo Ice Cream

Vegan oreo flavour ice cream made with oat milk, oreo, organic sugar and a twist of coconut

Mint choc Chip Ice Cream

This classic mint choc chip ice cream goes luxury with fresh mint leaves, dark chocolate, vanilla seeds, organic milk and organic cane sugar.

Rafello Ice Cream

The special bend of exotic vanilla flavour and gentle textures of Rafello in ice cream

Double Chocolate Ice Cream

Double chocolate ice cream is a combination of coca powder and melted chocolate with whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Pistacho Ice Cream

Our pistacho flavour is made with 100% Sicilian pistachos from the slope of Mt Etna. We only add organic milk, organic cane sugar and pure water.

Strawberry Ice Cream

A delicious strawberry ice cream is made with local wild strawberries, organic milk, whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Mango Ice Cream

Taste the delight that is our sugar free mango ice cream made with organic mangoes.

Bubble Gum Ice Cream

Our bubble gum ice cream is made with bubblegum extract, organic milk, whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Chocolate Orange Ice Cream

This is our another vegan chocolate orange ice cream made with chocolate, organic oranges, oat milk and organic cane sugar.
