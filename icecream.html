Scoops Icecream Cakes

Scoops

We make our delicious homemade gelato ice cream with lots of love and utmost care. Our ice cream is made daily on site in small batches, that allows us to provide with the funkiest and the freshest taste. We have created a variety of flavours ranging from the traditional Vanilla to Mint Choc Chip to many more exotic flavour of ice cream. We have got some exciting new vegan friendly flavours and all our ice creams are vegetarian.

Biscoff Ice Cream

Vegan biscoff ice cream is made with oat milk, organic cane sugar, topped with lotus sauce and lotus crumbs

Coconut Oreo Ice Cream

Vegan oreo flavour ice cream made with oat milk, oreo, organic sugar and a twist of coconut

Mint choc Chip Ice Cream

This classic mint choc chip ice cream goes luxury with fresh mint leaves, belgian dark chocolate, vanilla seeds, organic milk and organic cane sugar.

Raffaello Ice Cream

The special bend of exotic vanilla flavour, whole cream, organic caster sugar and gentle textures of Rafello chocolate makes this delicious ice cream

Double Chocolate Ice Cream

Double chocolate ice cream is a combination of coca powder and melted chocolate with whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Pistacho Ice Cream

Our pistacho flavour is made with 100% Sicilian pistachos from the slope of Mt Etna. We only add organic milk, organic cane sugar and pure water.

Strawberry Ice Cream

A delicious strawberry ice cream is made with local wild strawberries, organic milk, whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Mango Ice Cream

Taste the delight that is our sugar free mango ice cream made with organic mangoes.

Bubble Gum Ice Cream

Our bubble gum ice cream is made with bubblegum extract, organic milk, whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Chocolate Orange Ice Cream

This is our another vegan chocolate orange ice cream made with chocolate, organic oranges, oat milk and organic cane sugar.

Cappuccino Ice Cream

This ice cream is combination of grounded coffee, organic milk, whole cream and organic cane sugar that gives the flavour of cappuccino.

Toffee Popcorn Ice Cream

One of the flavours we made is Toffee Popcorn Ice cream, made with organic milk, whole cream, organic cane sugar and toffee popcorn.

Vanilla Ice Cream

Creamy intensively flavoured vanilla with a scent of bourbon vanilla from Madagascar, organic milk, whole cream and organic cane sugar.

Nutella Ice Cream

Vegan nutella ice cream made with oat milk, organic cane sugar and nutella.

Milkybar Ice Cream

A rich and creamy milky bar ice cream is combination of organic milk, whole cream, vanilla extract, organic cane sugar and milkybar chocolate.

Ferrero Rocher Ice Cream

Ferrero Rocher ice cream is like a dream, made with organic milk, organic caster sugar, vanilla extract, whole cream and ferrero rocher chocolate.

Chocolate Brownie Ice Cream

This vegan chocolate brownie ice-cream is made with coconut milk, organic dates, sweet potato and fresh vegan brownies.

Unicorn Ice Cream

Unicorn ice cream is a magical mix of bubble gum ice cream flavour and strawberry ice cream flavour.
